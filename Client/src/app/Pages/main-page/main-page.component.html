Dental Clinic

DentMedical

Dental treatments in all their complexity, including the application of dental implants and endodontics under a microscope, are performed in 16 offices. The fixed and mobile prosthetic works from various materials such as: acrylate, gold, composite, zirconium, ceramics, are made in our own laboratories and in collaboration with those from abroad. Backlit and panoramic radiographs are performed with modern equipment, with reduced doses of radiation. All these give us the possibility to perform top dental medical services, with promptness and professionalism, which generates satisfied patients.
